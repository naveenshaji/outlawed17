About OUTLAWED

Outlawed, a fest that saw it's beginning in the year 2006 has now become one of the biggest and most awaited cultural fests not only in the Law School circuit, but also among all undergraduate colleges in Eastern India and beyond.

From your run of the mill events to some very unconventional ones, we have in store for you the Great Kolkata Race, The Fashion Show, the Turncoat Debate, and Eastern and Western vocals, etc. which have drawn amazing talent and participation every year as well as some new never-seen-before events! This year, Outlawed 2019 guarantees to raise the stakes with the addition of splendid new events which will test your body, mind and soul.

Students across different colleges enjoy late night revels in the heart of Kolkata by attending the concerts of some of the biggest artists in the Indian music scene. In the course of the last couple of years, NUJS has had the delight of inviting bands like Indian Ocean, MIDIval Punditz Advaita, Prateek Kuhad, Karsh Kale Collectiv, Raghu Dixit Project, Indus Creed, Dualist Inquiry, Agnee, Parikrama and Nucleya.

So mark your calendars, and come to Outlawed’19 to win prizes, make new friends, and let yourself loose!

Registration Instructions



Registrations are now open. 
Deadline for Outstation Participants with accommodation – February 5, 2019
Deadline for Local Registrations without accommodation – February 8, 2019
Outstation Participants Registration with accommodation - Rs. 2500/- per person
Local Registrations without accommodation – Rs. 800/- per person

Payment has to be made through NEFT/RTGS transfer. Bank detail shall be mailed to you on enquiry through this form. On payment, the transferrer shall inform the Organising Committee of the payment details id and a breakup of the same. The details shall be sent to outlawed@nujs.edu

INCLUSIONS: The Outstation Participants Registration charge shall include accommodation for 4 nights, breakfast for 3 mornings, transport between accommodation and venue (bus) and registration for all events except the Great Kolkata Race, the Food Walk and the Battle of the Bands. The Local Registrations charge shall include registration charge for all events and starnites except the Great Kolkata Race, the Food Walk and the Battle of the Bands.

IDENTITIY: All Participants are required to carry valid government/university identity proofs. 

The Great Kolkata Race Registration - Rs. 350 per team. The team should consist of three people, non-negotiable. This event is capped at 15 teams. Only outstation participants can register.

The Food Walk Registration - Rs. 400 per person. The participants have to follow the trail strictly. This event is capped at 80 participants. Age no bar. Residence no bar. 

Battle of the Bands Registration - Rs. 500 per band. This is a screening-based event, all entries shall be sent to cultcom@nujs.edu. Age no bar. Residence no bar.

The festival shall be open to all participants till 8:00 PM, post 8:00 PM only people under 30 years of age can enter. THE ORGANISING COMMITTEE RESERVES ALL RIGHTS OF ADMISSION.
